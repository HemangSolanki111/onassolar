Three type of Solar Panel :

There are major three type of solar panel

1. Monocrystalline


(Image use from : https://www.solarreviews.com/blog/pros-and-cons-of-monocrystalline-vs-polycrystalline-solar-panels)
Monocrystalline panels are the most usage solar panels in rooftop solar panel.

Monocrystalline Solar panel is popular panel. it is mmost expensive option in all solar panel. it is high efficiency and best performance in all of other panel. but the higher cost of panel. cost of solar cell from single crystal, so it is also known as the single crystal panels. it can identify easily by there Black or Dark color.

2. Polycrystalline


(Image use from : https://www.solarreviews.com/blog/pros-and-cons-of-monocrystalline-vs-polycrystalline-solar-panels)
Polycrystalline is also unkown as “multi-crystalline”.

The Polycrystalline is considered as the mid-range panel. it s more affordable Solar Panel. it is also called “multi-crystalline”. polycrystalline cells are made from silicon. it cell are made from more fragments of crystal melted together. Polycrystalline panels used for residential home. it advatage is low cost but disadvantage is Lower efficiency and performance.

3. Thin-film


(Image use from : https://www.solarreviews.com/blog/pros-and-cons-of-monocrystalline-vs-polycrystalline-solar-panels)
Thin film solar Panel are mostly used in large industries solar installations because of their lower efficiency ratings.

Thin-Film solar panel is Portable and flexible advatage. its disadvantage is Lower efficiency and performance. Thin-film solar panel installations are less labor-intensive. it is made by thin layer of a photovoltaic substance. the manufacturing process, the photovoltaic substance forms a thin lightweight sheet that is, in some cases, flexible.
